Executive
Leadership
Support

For years people have been elevated into leadership roles for demonstrating excellence in areas of business that are not correlated with great leadership. Too many companies do not make the proper investment in developing their leaders. Many leaders are not even aware of their leadership style, the areas of their greatest contribution to the organization and the environments they create. The higher a person rises in a company the less likely they are to receive honest and candid feedback on their leadership effectiveness

Leadership
Development

Companies create gaps and weaknesses in their organization by relying too much upon their existing leaders to make decisions and dictate the course of action. Leaders need to be intentionally developed and environments must be created where people are empowered to make great decisions. We need to have great insight and understanding of the gifts and expertise of our people and how they contribute in the delivery of value to customers we serve. We need to create coaching, mentoring and environments of intentional apprenticeship if we want to insure sustainable high performance.

Sales
Excellence

To be excellent we must have ongoing development of the people who bring our value proposition to the marketplace. Too many companies consider sales training to be product information. Training programs make the mistake of focusing on transferring information without creating the environments for the people to apply, process and internalize the information. People move on emotion not logic.
